How to get Support

Where do you go to ask for help?
return to Experiences. Check out category A Check out category B

"I would say the one that has been maybe the most helpful so far was I joined the computer action team back when I started at PSU, and so that was really a great group of people who I felt like they were really open to being, like, oh, if you have any problems in your classes since Carla's classes are really tough, you can ask any of us. And I did end up making a couple of good friends there who I still talk to to this day, who I reach out to for support."

--CS Student of Color

What university support did you find helpful?
“The tutor program in the CS. When I [ had to repeat] 162, like I got an A in exams but freaked out in a demo. Demos I couldn’t pass. Demo day that, like, traumatized me. So then I really took advantage of the tutor program in the engineering building on the ground floor. [...]Then the 162, 163 classes had TAs. They were helpful, too.” --CS Student

"I just happened to fall in with a group of peers who were very, very helpful for getting through PSU. And have also been helpful on a professional level afterwards. I got both of the jobs that I have been in from peers. I just kind of walked into [the CS Lounge] and just started making friends and started talking with people and it really just went from there."

-- CS Student
